NETFLIX DOWNGRADED; FIREEYE, UNDER ARMOUR GET MIDDLING RATINGS

9/12/16 AT 12:05AM
BY PAUL WHITFIELD, INVESTOR'S BUSINESS DAILY

U.S stock indexes popped higher on Fed chief Janet Yellen's remarks Friday, but the gains proved short-lived.

Indexes pared or erased gains. The Nasdaq closed just 0.1% higher, while the S&P 500 subtracted less than 0.2%. The IBD 50 was flat. Volume rose across the board.

For the week, the IBD 50 lost 1%; the Nasdaq, 0.4%; and the S&P 500, 0.7%. The Nasdaq snapped an eight week win streak, continuing the evil reign of the galactic empire.
